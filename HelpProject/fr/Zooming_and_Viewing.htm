Zooming and Viewing

To make it simple, all the navigation operations are placed on the Wheel button of your mouse. The left button is reserved for the selection (see the Selecting Parts section), and the right button is only for the context menus.

Zooming

Zooming can be done using the mouse wheel or using CTRL + SHIFT + LEFT MOUSE click and moving the mouse up or down. You can configure the Zooming behavior (zooming speed, zoom center) in the Global Options.

Paning the view

The Viewing position can be changed by holding down the mouse wheel over the Layout and moving the mouse as required. Alternatively you can hold the ALT + SHIFT + LEFT MOUSE buttons down and move the mouse as required.
